Advantages of Split Learning

üîê Enhanced Privacy

Raw data never leaves the client device. Only intermediate representations are shared, making it extremely difficult to reconstruct original data.

üíæ Reduced Storage

Clients only store part of the model, significantly reducing memory requirements compared to full model training.

‚ö° Lower Computation

Computational load is distributed between client and server, making it feasible for resource-constrained devices.

More resistant to various privacy attacks compared to federated learning, as intermediate features are harder to exploit.
